Minute Breakfast Burrito

Description

A fast DIY breakfast for the road that's made in minutes with scrambled egg, salsa, and cheese wrapped up in a tortilla.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 465
  • Fat: 23g
  • Carbs: 41g
  • Protein: 25g

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 Tbsp Salsa
  • 1 Slice of American Cheese
  • 1 Tortilla

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Crack eggs into bowl
  3. Add salsa to bowl
  4. Stir ingredients together
  5. Microwave on high for 1 minute
  6. Stir again
  7. Microwave again for another minute (or until the mixture firms up)
  8. Place cheese and egg mixture on tortilla
  9. Wrap up tortilla
  10. Done!
