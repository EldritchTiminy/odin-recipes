Easy 5 Ingredient Chili

Original Author: tenastic66

Description

My father and I created this easy 5-ingredient chili recipe and everyone we have made it for loved it. It is fast, simple, and delicious.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 368
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbs: 35g
  • Protein: 33g

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef.
  • 1 (15 oz can) tomato sauce.
  • 1 (15 oz can) can kidney beans, drained.
  • 1 (15 oz can) can chili beans, not drained.
  • 2 tbsp chili powder, or to taste.
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sauce.
  4. Bring to a boil and stir in chili powder.
  5. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 15 minutes.
  6. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
